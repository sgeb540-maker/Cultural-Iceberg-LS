General information about this educational resource
Title The Cultural Iceberg Project: Understanding visible and invisible culture
Description The Cultural Iceberg is a project-based learning resource designed for 2nd year secondary students (2º ESO). Through the exploration of Commonwealth cultures, learners discover both visible and invisible aspects of culture using the Cultural Iceberg model. The project promotes intercultural awareness, critical thinking, communication skills and respect for diversity. Students analyse texts and videos, compare cultures, collaborate in groups and create a digital infographic to present their findings. The resource integrates digital tools such as Google Classroom, Canva and Genially while encouraging responsible, safe and creative use of technology.
